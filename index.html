01 Research direction

I care about the parts of intelligence that are hardest to specify.

My work asks how models can learn nuanced creative preferences rather than an averaged consensus; use multimodal context when it changes the answer; and be evaluated rigorously when tasks are subjective or under-specified.

I translate these questions into benchmarks, training objectives, and product-facing systems.
