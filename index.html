01 Research direction

I’m interested in the parts of intelligence that are difficult to reduce to a single right answer.

My work studies how models can learn individual creative preferences, use multimodal context when it genuinely changes an answer, and be evaluated carefully on subjective or under-specified tasks.

These questions take the form of benchmarks, training objectives, and product-facing systems.

A

Creative intelligence

Preference learning and personalized evaluation for open-ended generation.

B

Multimodal learning

Using vision, speech, and language where one modality is not enough.

C

Reliable evaluation

Benchmarks and explanation methods that make failure modes legible.
