About

I work on learning and evaluation for open-ended generative systems. At Adobe GenStudio, I build models and agentic systems for creative marketing content. My recent research looks at personalized creative judgment, robustness in multimodal models, and multilingual speech.

Previously, I was a research intern at Adobe Research and an NLP Research Engineer at ByteDance AI Lab. I received an M.S. in Intelligent Information Systems from Carnegie Mellon University and a B.Tech. from IIT Madras.

Research

I am broadly interested in how generative systems can become more useful when quality is subjective, context-dependent, or difficult to verify. My work spans creative generation and evaluation, multimodal learning, and the reliability of language models under distribution shift.
